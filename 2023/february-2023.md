# February 17

## LORD, I am stressed!

Australia smells like black amber and lavender; it gives me a sense of acute anxiety and adventure, seemingly cueing me that this is a different season from the ones before. It gives me a faint energy of excitement, teasing me with everything that lies ahead, feeding me a sense of hope and awakening the wild adventurer hidden inside of me.

Teasing me until I puke, I wonder: Will there be a time where I won't be constantly pushed out of my comfort zone? Pushed out without a safety net, instead standing on firm ground, unbothered, strong, stable, firm, and immovable.

Are these all under your play, Lord? To take me out into uncharted waters and lead me? If I may be honest, I feel like I'm Peter right now. I'm on the verge of drowning, crying as I reach out my hand to you. I am overwhelmed by uncertainties and "what ifs." Fear starts to cripple me, but with the knowledge I have of you, I battle and subdue it, knowing full well that fear does not come from the Lord.

The Lord has already done everything to give me an abundant life and have it to the full. Not a life that is left from crumbs, but one that is blessed by God to bless others as well.
