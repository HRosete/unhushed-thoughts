# 28 February

## For Such A Time As This

Wars loom on all fronts;<br>
My innards shiver.<br>
You then held me tight,<br>
and brought peace like a river.

You armed me with quiver and bow;<br>
Instructed me to keep real close.<br>
"My Word will be your arrow,<br>
Without me, you'll lose."

"Let me fight this battle,<br>
This was not yours to win.<br>
Keep in pace with me; don't delay,<br>
Victory's ushered by the light of day."
