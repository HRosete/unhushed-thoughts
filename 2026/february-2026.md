# 28 February

## For Such A Time As This

Wars loom on all fronts;
My innards shiver.
You then held me tight,
and brought peace like a river.

You quipped me with quiver and bow;
Instructed me to keep real close.
"My Word will be your arrow,
Without me, you'll lose."

"Let me fight this battle,
This was not yours to win.
Keep in pace with me; don't delay,
Victory's ushered by the light of day."
